The Republic of Glamoth was once a thriving interstellar civilization, protected by its legendary Iron Cavalry. At the heart of their military might was Project SAM, a series of biomechanical soldiers designed to interface directly with Stellaron energy. Subject AR-26710, later known as Firefly, was among the final batch of SAM units produced before the catastrophe.


The Swarm Disaster struck without warning. Insectoid entities of unknown origin descended upon Glamoth, consuming everything in their path. Queen Titania, the hive mind commanding the Swarm, proved immune to conventional weaponry. The Iron Cavalry was annihilated in 72 hours. In a desperate final gambit, the remaining SAM units were deployed in a suicide charge against the Queen. AR-26710 was the sole survivor.


The victory came at a terrible cost. Prolonged exposure to Stellaron energy during the battle triggered Entropy Loss Syndrome, a degenerative condition that slowly erases the host's physical form. Her body is literally fading from existence, cell by cell. Current estimates suggest her remaining operational lifespan is measured in months, not years.


Rescued from the ruins of Glamoth by Elio, she was given a new identity and purpose within the Stellaron Hunters. The name Firefly was chosen by the subject herself, a reference to the creatures she had read about in old Earth literature. Like their namesake, she burns brightly knowing her light will soon be extinguished.
